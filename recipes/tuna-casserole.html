Tuna, macaroni, creamy soup, cheese and fried onions are all you need to make this super easy tuna casserole that I learned from my roommate. It's great as leftovers, too.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked macaroni
  • tuna, drained
  • condensed cream of chicken soup
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • french fried onions
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine macaroni, tuna, and soup. Mix well and then top with cheese
  3. Bake at 350 degrees F for about 25 minutes, or until bubbly. Sparkle with fried onions, and bake for another 5 minutes. Serve hot.
